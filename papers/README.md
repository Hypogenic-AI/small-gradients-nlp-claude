# Downloaded Papers

## Core Model Collapse Papers

1. **The Curse of Recursion: Training on Generated Data Makes Models Forget** (papers/shumailov2023_curse_of_recursion.pdf)
   - Authors: Shumailov et al.
   - Year: 2023
   - arXiv: 2305.17493
   - Why relevant: Foundational model collapse paper; explicitly mentions "very small gradients" from synthetic data

2. **AI models collapse when trained on recursively generated data** (papers/shumailov2024_ai_models_collapse_nature.pdf)
   - Authors: Shumailov et al.
   - Year: 2024
   - arXiv: 2401.00368
   - Why relevant: Nature version of the model collapse paper

3. **Is Model Collapse Inevitable?** (papers/dohmatob2024_model_collapse_inevitable.pdf)
   - Authors: Dohmatob et al.
   - Year: 2024
   - arXiv: 2404.01413
   - Why relevant: Shows accumulating real data can break the curse of recursion

4. **A Tale of Tails: Model Collapse as a Change of Scaling Laws** (papers/gerstgrasser2024_tale_of_tails.pdf)
   - Authors: Dohmatob, Feng, Yang, Charton, Kempe
   - Year: 2024
   - arXiv: 2402.07043
   - Why relevant: Theoretical framework connecting tail truncation to scaling law degradation

5. **How Bad is Training on Synthetic Data?** (papers/kazdan2024_how_bad_synthetic_data.pdf)
   - Authors: Kazdan, Rushing, Goldfeld
   - Year: 2024
   - arXiv: 2404.05090
   - Why relevant: Statistical analysis of collapse under different data mixing paradigms

6. **Strong Model Collapse** (papers/dohmatob2024_strong_model_collapse.pdf)
   - Authors: Dohmatob, Feng, Kempe
   - Year: 2024
   - arXiv: 2410.04840
   - Why relevant: Shows even 1% synthetic contamination can cause collapse

7. **Theoretical Proof that AR Language Models Collapse** (papers/wang2024_theoretical_proof_collapse.pdf)
   - Authors: Wang et al.
   - Year: 2024
   - arXiv: 2412.14872
   - Why relevant: First proof of collapse specifically for auto-regressive LMs
